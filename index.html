Sagrada Familia

What We Do

ESTUPENDO is a community group for Manchester residents looking to start or continue learning the Spanish language and/or culture. We organise weekly meetups where members can meet and mingle with others interested in learning Spanish, along with Hispanic and Latin members of the community.

Meetups take place in casual settings in the city centre, and consist of a variety of fun and engaging social activities. Or, you could just sit and have a drink.

Why We Do It

"If you talk to a man in a language he understands, that goes to his head. If you talk to him in his own language, that goes to his heart."

We are firm believers that studying a foreign language is one of the most enriching hobbies a person can have. At ESTUPENDO, we aim to provide a platform for anyone of any age and skill level to broaden their horizons and learn to speak Spanish.
